Double Cheeseburger Recipe

Description

This is a double cheeseburger. It contains a bun, followed by meat, followed by cheese, followed by more meat, followed by more cheese, followed by more bun. The doubling of all ingredients gives it the name double cheeseburger. They are very good.

Ingredients

  • Top Bun
  • Cheese
  • Burger
  • Cheese
  • Burger
  • Bottom Bun

Steps

  1. Place bottom bun
  2. Place Burger
  3. Place Cheese
  4. Place Burger
  5. Place Cheese
  6. Place Top Bun
